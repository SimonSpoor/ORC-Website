Who Are We?

Okanagan Rover Craft aims to foster an environment of continued learning in the robotics and mechatronics industry by building a rover to compete at the Canadian International Rover Challenge (CIRC)! We are a design-oriented student association at the University of British Columbia Okanagan, who design, build, and pilot mars-style rovers. Our organization seeks to provide a practical and fun experience to our members as we hone our skills as engineers and young professionals. Welcome to our website!
