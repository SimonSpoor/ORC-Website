About Us

Originally a shared dream of a few classmates, Okanagan Rover Craft was formally established in October of 2025, and has been firing on all cylinders ever since. Okanagan Rover Craft is composed of a community of young professionals with a team goal to build the best rover possible to compete at the Canadian International Robotics Challenge (CIRC). Okanagan Rover Craft is more than just a project, it’s an opportunity to build relationships, grow as professionals, and have fun. Okanagan Rover Craft is always looking for skilled and enthusiastic new members, reach out to us to learn more about the Okanagan Rovercraft team!

How to get involved

Looking to get involved in the future of Okanagan Rover Craft? We are always looking for passionate collaborators to join our mission. If you represent an organization looking to empower a team of young professionals, our outreach team would be thrilled to discuss the benefits of partnering with Okanagan Rover Craft with you! Sponsoring our team provides your organization with brand visibility while directly supporting our team's goals. A partnership with Okanagan Rover Craft allows you to connect with top student talent in STEM fields and support both our craft, and our team's future. If you are interested in supporting us, check out our sponsorship page!
